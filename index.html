A Timeline of Public Interest Hacks

Tracing the emergence and evolution of politically motivated hack-and-leak operations — from precursors in the early 2000s through their stabilization as a geopolitical strategy.

Based on Gabriella Coleman, "The Public Interest Hack" (Limn, 2017)

Precursor
Hacktivist PIH
Mysterious / Obscured Motive
Suspected State Actor
Coleman argues the PIH did not exist as a recognizable political strategy before ~2007 and was largely stabilized by Anonymous in 2011. The "mysterious" species — where motives are concealed — emerged in 2014 and has since been adopted for statecraft and geopolitical manipulation.
